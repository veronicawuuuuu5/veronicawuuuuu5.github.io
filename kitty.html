GeeGee


This is my cat GeeGee. Look at this cat model! He actually looks a lot more grumpy on a regular day, had to pose for this as I was holding a treat behind the camera. Tough life even as a cat.


I have always considered myself a dog person my whole life until I met GeeGee. I used to hate cats for some reason, I was scared of the scratches and all those online videos I saw with cats attacking their owners out of nowhere. But GeeGee is honestly the sweestest little kitty you will ever meet!


GeeGee is a very nonchalant boy, he would head bump me every morning, beg for some scratches on his head and do his kneading business. He likes belly rub the most and being held up like a baby, which makes me wonder if he low key knows I like dogs.


Of course being a cutie he is, he is spoiled by many of my friends with treats and toys and a self-portrait from his auntie.
