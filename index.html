About Isaac Daniel Eng

Isaac Daniel Eng is the Fiddler on the Loop!Best known for his looping covers, Isaac creates live orchestral arrangaments with a violin and effects pedals. Isaac's career as a performer and educator have brought him around the world and back again. Most noteably, Isaac taught music at the Jin Cai International School in Shanghai for 2 years. Aside from teaching, Isaac performed with Shanghai rock band Dahaha. Together they toured China releasing two original albums titled "Dakou Radio" and "The Niu Testament". Isaac is classically trained and completed a Bachelor of Music Education at Western University. He then pursued a Master of Teaching at the University of Toronto and is certified with the Ontario College of Teachers.
